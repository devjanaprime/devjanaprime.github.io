Song 1: Whiskey, Coffee, Water

About

  • tune down to Eb
  • only 2 riffs really, dynamics and rhythm change
  • if you'd like to do vocals, the "ahh"s in call/response of "to the next drop" is fair game
  • also sing along with chorus:
  • "Gates are open I must away
  • All attempts to containthe shape
  • Always end up with us just flailing
  • As authority is bailing"

Structure:

  • Intro (verse riff 2x guitar only, 2x with bass/rGuitar tease)
  • verse 1 (8x, half time feel)
  • chorus
  • preVerse (verse riff 4x, new feel)
  • verse 2 (8x)
  • chorus
  • preVerse (verse riff 4x, double time)
  • verse 3 (8x)
  • outro (verse riff 4x, rGuitar/bass call/response, half time)
  • outro (verse riff 4x, rGuitar/bass full riff, toms)
